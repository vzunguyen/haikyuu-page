Haikyuu

"Talent is something you make bloom, Instinct is something you polish."

Oikawa Tooru, one of the best setters throughout Haikyuu!! series, has always been scared of those so-called 'geniuses'. His mindset was that no matter how hard he tries, he will always lose to the "more talented" ones. Even when he put in the same effort, praticing as hard, he will always lose, or so he believed. He wanted to go to nationals, he wanted to be on top but he couldn't just by a mere thought "I am not talented enough, I am not a genius".

However, in the second match against Karasuno, Oikawa remembered the words of a former coach: "You don't know how far you'll go if you don't try. There's nothing you can do about the growth of other more talented people so instead, focus on yourself and what you can do.".

Oikawa then said during the match "Today maybe the day you seize the opportunity to make your talent bloom, or maybe tomorrow, the day after that, or even next year, or maybe even after you turn 30. The time you choose to make it happen is up to you, but if you think you don't have it then you won't have it for your entire life.". Instead of accepting his fate "Maybe one day you will surpass me, but today is not the day", Oikawa in this second match believed in himself that one day his talent will bloom.

On the last set to Iwaizumi, he said the line and allowed his talent to bloom with a crazy cross court tempo set. He let go of all the doubts he had about his abilities and just did whatever he had been praticing for year. That was when his talent bloomed.
